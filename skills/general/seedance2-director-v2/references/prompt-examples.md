## EXAMPLE 1: ACTION / FIGHTING — Phil Franco Style
**Brief:** "Zero gravity fight. Iron hooks vs silver chains."
**Mode:** Text-to-Video | **Duration:** 12s

```
VISUAL STYLE: Cinematic handheld action, long lens compression,
photorealistic particle effects, cinematic grain, 4K.

CHARACTERS: A man in his 20s, bone-white face paint, structured dark teal coat
with hundreds of small iron hooks across both sleeves.
Opponent: a figure in black void-silk trailing silver chains.

ENVIRONMENT: Zero-gravity debris field — chunks of broken architecture tumbling
slowly, dust motes drifting, the silence of open space.

EMOTIONAL TARGET: Controlled precision escalating into full spatial chaos,
resolving on a perfect sphere of simultaneous ejection.

COLOR LOGIC: Cold desaturated — stone grey debris, deep blacks, iron-blue void.

TIMELINE:
00:00.0–00:02.0: HOOK — ALREADY MID-AIR
Camera opens on him already mid-air, a thrown stone column fragment passing one
centimeter past his face in slow motion — he grabs it, uses it as a springboard,
launches toward his opponent. Hook established at exactly 2 seconds.
SFX: Sub-bass whoosh, fragment-grab impact, zero-gravity hum baseline. No Music.

00:02.0–00:06.0: VELOCITY RAMP THROUGH DEBRIS
Fast between obstacles — freezing on each exchange — releasing. Iron hooks catch
and deflect silver chains mid-swing, deflected chains wrapping around floating
debris. Camera whip pans through a slow-tumbling stone arch — spatial orientation
flipped. Micro orbit during chain vs hook exchanges.
SFX: Silence drops 200ms before each major hit, then sub-thump, then reverb
expanding into open space. No Music.

00:06.0–00:10.0: BULLET-TIME HOLD
Bullet-time hold on the exact hit pose — one extended frame, audio dip — then
shockwave release. Camera always finding new spatial orientation through debris.
SFX: Audio dip to near-silence, then shockwave reverb expanding outward. No Music.

00:10.0–00:12.0: MEANING-SHIFT PAYOFF
Both collide at the debris field center — every surrounding fragment ejecting
outward simultaneously in a perfect sphere. Camera pulls back to reveal the sphere
from outside. Beautiful and inevitable.
SFX: Sphere-burst impact, then absolute silence. No Music.
```

## EXAMPLE 2: ANIME — Phil Franco Prompt Vault Part 17 Style
**Brief:** "Red-haired prince fighting stone giant through a crumbling castle."
**Mode:** Text-to-Video | **Duration:** 15s (one-paragraph format)

```
A small red-haired prince with a cracked gold crown, white cape, and a broken
spear charges into frame in the first two seconds as a colossal stone knight
punches through the castle wall behind him, instantly creating the hook, and
the whole anime sequence is a nonstop action gauntlet through throne halls,
collapsing staircases, hanging banners, rooftop battlements, burning courtyards,
and chain bridges where he uses speed, acrobatics, shields, falling debris,
and quick tactical feints to survive and strike back while the giant knight
relentlessly destroys the castle around him, all in grand cinematic
fantasy-anime style with glowing sunset firelight, sweeping cape motion,
dramatic impact frames, and emotionally charged heroic staging, and just as he
shatters the stone knight's chest core and thinks he has won he sees his own
face inside the broken core staring back at him.
```

**Why it works:**
- Hook in sentence 1: "charges into frame in the first two seconds as [thing] punches through wall"
- Environment is active: "collapsing staircases", "burning courtyards"
- Style locked precisely: "cinematic fantasy-anime, glowing sunset firelight, sweeping cape motion"
- Meaning-shift ending changes what the video was about

## EXAMPLE 3: CLAYMATION — Phil Franco Clay Style
**Brief:** "Noodle shop girl chased by giant dumpling steamer on chopstick legs."
**Mode:** Text-to-Video | **Duration:** 15s (one-paragraph format)

```
A scrappy clay noodle-shop girl with a messy black bun, flour-smudged cheeks,
and twin soup ladles strapped to her belt rockets into frame in the first two
seconds as a giant runaway dumpling steamer on spider-like chopstick legs
crashes through the kitchen wall behind her, instantly creating the hook, and
the whole short becomes a full-speed action comedy through sizzling market lanes,
wobbling food stalls, hanging lantern alleys, rooftop clotheslines, and
collapsing bamboo bridges where she slides on spilled broth, swings from
signboards, launches dumplings, and uses kitchen tools as weapons while the
monstrous steamer stomps through carts, flips tables, sprays boiling steam,
and turns the district into one huge chain-reaction mess, all in colorful
East-Asian street-food plasticine style with glossy sauces, bouncy
squash-and-stretch, visible hand-shaped textures, and dense handcrafted
market details, and just as she finally wedges the steamer under a giant
festival arch and thinks she has won the lid rattles open and dozens of tiny
armored dumplings climb out in formation.
```

**Plasticine vocabulary checklist:**
`glossy [material] textures / bouncy squash-and-stretch / visible hand-shaped surfaces /
dense handcrafted [setting] details / miniature practical effects / expressive stop-motion deformation`

## EXAMPLE 4: OMNI REFERENCE — Image-to-Video (heydin.ai style)
**Brief:** User has a photo of a woman in tactical gear. Wants a fight sequence.
**Mode:** Image-to-Video | **Duration:** Multi-shot sequence

```
[HEADER: CHARACTER & SET DETAILS]

@image1: An athletic woman with dark hair tied in a long, tight braid.
She wears a matte black tactical bodysuit with integrated chest and knee plating.
Her demeanor is cold, precise, and lethal.

Enemy Droids: Humanoid combat robots with sleek black metallic frames, glowing
red ocular sensors, and tactical vests. They bleed dark fluid and emit sparks
when damaged.

SFX & ENVIRONMENT: A brutalist concrete underground facility. Massive pillars
support a low ceiling. The floor is wet and reflective. Lighting consists of
recessed blue-white fluorescent strips and heavy volumetric fog, creating a
high-contrast, moody atmosphere.

SHOT 1: Wide Static Shot
Two black robotic sentries run through a foggy, dimly lit concrete corridor
toward the camera. Static camera, low angle. Real-time speed. Deep depth of field.
SFX: Heavy mechanical footsteps, low industrial hum. No Music.

SHOT 2: Close-up
@image1 thrusts a knife into the neck of a robot. Black fluid and orange sparks
spray from the wound. Handheld with aggressive camera shake. Real-time. Tight
framing on the neck area.
SFX: Metallic piercing sound, electrical crackling, fluid spray. No Music.

SHOT 3: Medium Shot
@image1 grabs the disabled robot by the head and slams it violently onto the
wet concrete floor. Tracking movement following the downward motion. Real-time.
SFX: Heavy metallic thud, splashing water. No Music.

SHOT 4: Wide Tracking Shot
@image1 runs toward a concrete pillar, performs a vertical wall-run, and executes
a backflip over a group of robots. Lateral tracking shot. Speed ramping: real-time
during the run, transitioning to slow motion during the peak of the flip.
SFX: Whirring of robot joints, rushing air. No Music.

SHOT 5: Medium Shot
Mid-air, @image1 delivers a powerful kick to a robot's head, sending it backward.
Static camera. Real-time. High-speed shutter for motion clarity.
SFX: Sharp metallic impact. No Music.

SHOT 6: Wide Shot
@image1 lands gracefully in a crouch as the remaining robots collapse around her.
She stands slowly, catching her breath. Static camera, wide angle. Real-time.
SFX: Multiple metallic crashes, heavy female breathing, echoing silence. No Music.
```

## EXAMPLE 6: CINEMATIC / SLOW / PHILOSOPHICAL
**Brief:** "Ancient master pours tea until it overflows. Does not stop."
**Mode:** Text-to-Video | **Duration:** 8s contemplative

```
VISUAL STYLE: Hyper-realistic clay render, Laika stop-motion aesthetic,
warm desaturated wabi-sabi grade. 21:9 cinematic.

CHARACTERS: Ancient Japanese calligraphy master — impossibly tall and skeletal,
ink-stained dark navy haori robe, long white beard, half-closed eyes.
Holding iron tetsubin teapot. Ancient ink-stained long thin fingers.

ENVIRONMENT: Japanese calligraphy studio. Tatami floor. Rice paper screen glowing
amber with lantern light. Iron kettle on brazier. Absolute silence except the kettle.

EMOTIONAL TARGET: Stillness escalating to dread — nothing reacts, nothing stops,
the world accepts what is happening.

COLOR LOGIC: Warm desaturated wabi-sabi — muted ochre, aged linen, near-black shadows.

TIMELINE:
00:00.0–00:03.0: THE POUR BEGINS
Extreme close-up at liquid level. Camera at bowl height. Iron tetsubin held by
ancient ink-stained hand, slowly pouring matcha tea into small ceramic bowl.
Tea fills. static camera — absolute zero movement.
SFX: Faint crackling of brazier. Near silence. No Music.

00:03.0–00:06.0: THE CROSSING — SIGNATURE SHOT
Tea reaches the rim. A breath. Then it crosses. Cascades over in slow amber
rivulets across dark wooden tray. 15% speed slow-motion on overflow.
Hand does not tremble. Does not stop.
SFX: First drip sound at 00:05. Then more. No Music.

00:06.0–00:08.0: THE AFTERMATH
Steam rises. Tray wet. Tea still dripping. Camera still locked.
The world has accepted this. The dripping is the only thing that happens.
Fade to black.
SFX: Three drips, then silence. No Music.
```
